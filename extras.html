Our purpose


The purpose of Calendaily is to bring students together without being affected by class schedules

With Calendaily you can see what time is best to be able to meet and have a good time together


About us

We began our journey because we, as students, saw a problem among our own, it was difficult to know when the schedules of our friends or teammates could allow us to get together

That is why we decided to create Calendaily, this way we can know the available times all of our friends have, and have meeteing at times when everybody can
